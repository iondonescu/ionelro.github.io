bubu

Bubu is a domestic cat and this are some facts about it:

  • Food: Mice, Pizza and cat food
  • Weigh: 4 to 5 kg
  • Height: about 23–25 cm
  • Lifespan: 12–15 years

The timing of Bubu activity is quite flexible and varied, which means it is more active in the morning and evening, as a response to our activity at these times.
